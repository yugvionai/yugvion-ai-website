About YUGVION AI

YUGVION AI is focused on building intelligent digital solutions that help businesses and students grow in the modern technology era.

We specialize in website development, AI automation, business analytics, and academic technology projects.

Our goal is to combine innovation, data, and automation to deliver practical and scalable digital solutions.

At YUGVION AI, we believe in three core values: Intelligence, Trust, and Growth.

I am Preetham C, the founder of YUGVION AI and an MBA graduate specializing in Business Analytics and Human Resource Management.

I come from a non-technical background, and my journey into technology started with curiosity. Like many people, I once thought artificial intelligence was only for programmers and computer experts.

Today, many people see AI as something complicated or even something to fear, believing it will replace humans. But my perspective is different.

I see AI not as a competitor, but as a guide, a colleague, an assistant, and even a friend that can help us think better, work smarter, and create new possibilities.

This belief inspired me to start YUGVION AI, with the vision of bridging the gap between non-technical ideas and advanced technology.

YUGVION AI builds intelligent digital solutions including AI automation, web development, and analytics systems for businesses and students.
